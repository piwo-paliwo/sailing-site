## Day 1
**Bogaczewo (Zielona Zatoka) - Sztynort**

We set off around 10:00. Our destination is the marina at Sztynort (loud/noisy piers).

~~At 21:00 a broadcast of the World Cup final match Spain - Argentina is planned (*vamos*)~~

~~At 23:00 the 1st round of the Captains' Cup in "flanki" (a card game) is planned.~~

Around 13:00, an incident occurred on the yacht Sztos involving a broken mast. Because of this, the yacht was forced to return to the port of Zielona Zatoka.

![IMG_9199 Medium](IMG_9199Big.png)

Meanwhile, the crew of the yacht Vabank went off for a swim and had lunch in a romantic little bay on Lake Kisajno. Afterwards, in solidarity with the shipwreck survivors, they stayed at the nearest port - Łabędzi Ostrów.

![IMG_9210 Medium](IMG_9210Big.png)

## Day 2

At 10:30 the crew of the yacht Sztos removed the broken mast from the boat and replaced a brake pad, then at 14:00 they pumped out the sewage tank and headed to the Rybaczówka marina in Bogaczewo.

![IMG_9214 Medium](IMG_9214Big.png) 

Dinner was served at 17:00 in the form of a huge sturgeon.
